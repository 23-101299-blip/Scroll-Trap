The Great Disconnect

Modern phones while supposedly connecting us and giving us a world of information at our fingertips, can lead to social isolation. Heavy social media use can often contribute to mental health issues like anxiety, depression, and loneliness. It is vital to find a balance between digital and physical connections to lead a healthier life.

Nomophobia

Nomophobia, or NOMOBILE-PHONE-PHOBIA, is the psychological fear or anxiety of being without your mobile phone or losing mobile service. It often stems from a dependency on the device for communication, social validation, and information, leading to feelings of panic and irritability when the phone is not accessible.
